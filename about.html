ABOUT US

We are four NYC women dedicated to benefitting the wellbeing of poor Black and Latinx women in Harlem, NYC through education and accessible empowerment tools. It is our hope that these tools and resources will provide relief to a demographic that experiences the most serious psychological distress (SPD) in the city
